In this project, we developed **Job Finder**, an application designed to help job seekers discover, track, and apply to positions efficiently.  
Key features include:  
- Searching through job listings by keywords, location, or filters.  
- Viewing job details and tracking applications over time.  
- Managing a personalized dashboard to monitor progress and opportunities.
